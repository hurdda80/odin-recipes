Dill Pickle Dip

A delicious dill pickle dip everyone wants to scoop onto a cracker

Quick and easy, sure to be a hit with everybody!

Ingredients

  • 8OZ cream cheese at room temp
  • 1 cup chopped dill pickles
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped sweet onion
  • 2TBS pickle juice
  • 1TSP dried dill weed
  • 1/2TSP kosher salt
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Stir cream cheese in a bowl with a wooden sppon until smooth
  2. Stir in dill pickles, onion, pickle juice, dill weed, salt, and pepper until evenly distributed
  3. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour
  4. Serve and enjoy
